CHARACTERS IN NOLI ME TANGERE

Maria Clara
  • The "daughter" of Kapitan Tiago
    and Dona Pia Alba.
  • Biological father is Padre Damaso
    but was raised by Kapitan Tiago.
  • First appeared in Chapter 5 of Noli Me Tangere,
    "Pangarap sa Gabing Madilim."

Kapitan Tiago
  • The richest man known in San Diego.
  • Is not a true man of God
    because he uses his high-ranking position as an advantage
    and pays his way through.

Tiya Isabel
  • Acted as Maria Clara's mother figure
    after Dona Pia Alba (biological mother)
    passed away.
  • Kapitan Tiago's cousin and Maria Clara's aunt

Basilio
  • 10-year old son of Sisa and Pedro;
    brother of Crispin.
  • Got shot at by guard civils.

Crispin
  • 7-year old son of Sisa and Pedro;
    brother of Basilio
  • Stole money and got punished by the Sakristan.
