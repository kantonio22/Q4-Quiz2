Top 3 Destinations in the Philippines

The Philippines, a southeast Asian country, is very popular for their tropical weather and al fresco ambiance. However, we all are way too familiar with the beaches already, how about Philippines' must-visit hills and mountains?

Chocolate Hills, Bohol

As soon as the hot weather hits, the grass on these beautiful hills turn brown,resulting into "chocolate kisses."

Banaue Rice Terraces

The Banaue Rice Terraces are a National Cultural Treasure of the Philippines and are considered to be the 8th Natural Wonder of the World by many Filipinos.

Mayon Volcano

It is known for its perfectly conical shape whose natural beauty has inspired a number of legends and art.
